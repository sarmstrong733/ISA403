Frequently Asked Questions

  • albumWhat is Solo Penguin Records?
    Solo Penguin Records is a label that not only supports its artists, but unlike most records, works to build relationships and improve them both personally as well as professionally. It is our goal to inspire and give back.
  • albumHow does Solo Penguin Records choose its artists?
    Here at Solo Penguin Records, we strive to sign the best and most unique artists who are also exceptional individuals outstide the spotlight. It is our goal to produce fantastic music for a diverse audience and make the world a better place while doing it.
  • albumWhen did Solo Penguin Records establish itself?
    Solo Penguin Records was established in September of 2018. It began with a small team of 3 music-lovers and has grown to be one of the most well known record labels to date.
  • albumHow can I be signed by Solo Penguin Records?
    Solo Penguin Records employs an elite group of scouts that will find the diamonds in the rough. If you make great music and share it with the world, there's a high chance we will notice you. Unfotunately, we do not accept or listen to unsolicited material sent our way.
  • albumWhich artists have Solo Penguin Records signed?
    Solo Penguin Records has a myriad of partnerships that span a variety of genres. Some of these include Justin Bieber, Rihanna, Taylor Swift, and Beyonce. The number is always growing and we are excited to see which artists join us in the future.
  • albumHow can I contact Solo Penguin Records with direct questions?
    If our FAQs have not answered your questions, please navigate to the Contact Us page for further assistance.
