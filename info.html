Our Story

How we got started.

Solo Penguin Records strives to better its employees as much as its partners. For this reason, being on a team here is not like being on a team somewhere else. We nurture self-sustainable teams that inspire its members to be better without external pressures. Personal and professional development are taken very seriously, and it can be seen every day inside and outside the office.

Culture

Being part of the family.

When you work for Solo Penguin Records, you do not work for a company. Rather, you join a family who shares the same passions and interests as you. We take our culture seriously here because in the end, it's the people from the bottom to the top who make a great company.

Team

Working on a team.

It all began in 2018 with 3 ambitious individuals, a love for music, and a passion for making the world a better place. In the early days, Solo Penguin Records grew with a purpose. We wanted a way to share what we believed to be exceptional music with the world and build relationships while doing it. After setting the building blocks through our mission, little by little we went from a 3 person operation to a global record label.

Jobs

Joining us.

If you aspire to work for a company like Solo Penguin Records, we are always looking for new members of the family who are perfect fits for the culture we have created. If you think that might be you, navigate to the Contact Us page to learn more information.
