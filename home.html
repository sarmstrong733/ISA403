We Are 

Solo 

Penguin Records

Established in September of 2018 by 3 ambitious individuals whose appreciation and passion for music was finally ready to meet the world. The love we have for music permeates everything that we do and has driven every decision we make. We have carefully built a label full of people that share these same values and that has pushed us to where we are today. It is our goal to produce fantastic music for a diverse audience and make the world a better place while doing it. We're glad that you've made it to our site and are excited for you to see all the talent our artists have!
